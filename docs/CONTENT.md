🔒 Section label: "What we help with"

🔒 Headline (with rotating word animation):

🔒 Intro:
> Not posts. Not impressions. Not "thought leadership." Those are by-products. The real work is putting your expertise — or your team's — in front of the right people, consistently enough that they remember you when the buying moment comes.

### Six cards (all 🔒):

**01. Sound like you — not like everyone else**
Personal-voice LinkedIn content extracted from your actual thinking. No AI filler, no recycled advice. The kind of posts your audience replies to because they recognise something real.

**02. Long-form that pulls its weight**
Blogs and articles that show your thinking in full — so the right people Google you, find depth, and have a reason to reach out. SEO-aware, AI-search-friendly, written to be read.

**03. Newsletters & email that stay close**
Monthly or fortnightly newsletters that keep you front of mind with the people who've already raised their hand. Built in your tone, repurposed from your existing content so nothing's wasted.

**04. The right people in your network**
Targeted connection campaigns inside your actual Ideal Client Profile, so the content lands in front of decision-makers — not the same 500 contacts on rotation.

**05. Numbers that mean something**
Monthly Momentum Reviews showing what worked, what didn't, and what's next. Not vanity dashboards — clear reads on the content, the network, and the conversations it's creating.

**06. Activate the right team members**
For companies: pick the salespeople, consultants, or partners who should be the human voice of the business. We turn them into trust builders — without adding to their workload.

## How it works

🔒 Section label: "How it works"
🔒 Headline: "A simple system you barely have to touch."

🔒 Sub: "You're busy running the business. We built the process so your input is minimal — a few voice notes a month — and the output is consistent, on-brand content that compounds."

### Four steps (all 🔒):

**01. We extract your thinking**
Through the Expert Voice Dashboard, you answer simple, structured questions that pull out your experience, opinions, and insights. Answer by voice note in the car, between meetings, on a walk — whatever's easiest. No writing required.

**02. We build your content engine**
From those answers, we craft LinkedIn posts in your voice, long-form blogs that show your depth, and the supporting content that keeps everything connected. You review, give feedback, and we schedule.

**03. We grow visibility & conversations**
Targeted connection campaigns expand your network inside your actual ICP. Your content lands in front of decision-makers who didn't know you existed last month. Steady, consistent traffic to your profile and website.

**04. We review & refine each month**
Monthly strategy calls and Momentum Reviews look at what's working, what topics landed, where conversations are happening, and where to push next. The system gets sharper every month.
