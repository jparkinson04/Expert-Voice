How it works

A simple system you barely have to touch.

You're busy running the business. We built the process so your input is minimal — a few voice notes a month — and the output is consistent, on-brand content that compounds.

See the full process →
01

We extract your thinking

Through the Expert Voice Dashboard, you answer simple, structured questions that pull out your experience, opinions, and insights. Answer by voice note in the car, between meetings, on a walk — whatever's easiest. No writing required.

02

We build your content engine

From those answers, we craft LinkedIn posts in your voice, long-form blogs that show your depth, and the supporting content that keeps everything connected. You review, give feedback, and we schedule.

03

We grow visibility & conversations

Targeted connection campaigns expand your network inside your actual ICP. Your content lands in front of decision-makers who didn't know you existed last month. Steady, consistent traffic to your profile and website.

04

We review & refine each month

Monthly strategy calls and Momentum Reviews look at what's working, what topics landed, where conversations are happening, and where to push next. The system gets sharper every month.
